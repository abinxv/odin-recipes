Strawberry Milkshake

Description

A Strawberry Milkshake is a perfect summer treat. It's my most favorite shake to make. It takes just a few minutes to prepare and is best served cold.

Ingredients

For 1 cup:
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash the strawberries and cut them into small pieces.
  2. Pour milk, strawberries, and a scoop of vanilla ice cream into a blender.
  3. Add sugar if you prefer a sweeter taste.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Pour into a cup and enjoy!
