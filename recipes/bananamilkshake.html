Protein Banana Shake

Description

A Protein Banana Shake is a nutritious and energy-boosting drink, perfect for breakfast or post-workout. It takes less than 5 minutes to make and is best served chilled.

Ingredients

For 1 cup:
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup milk (or almond milk)
  • 1 scoop protein powder (vanilla or chocolate)
  • 3-4 ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel and slice the banana into small pieces.
  2. Pour milk, banana slices and protein powder into a blender.
  3. Add ice cubes for a chilled texture.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Pour into a cup and enjoy!
