Apple Milkshake

Description

An Apple Milkshake is a healthy yet tasteful drink to have whenever you would like. It is a great dessert to have after dinner. It is an easy recipe with only 3 ingredients and takes less than 5 minutes to make. Best served chilled.

Ingredients

For 1 cup:
  • 1 apple
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Cut apple into small 1 inch cubes
  2. Pour milk,cut apple pieces and scoop of vanilla ice cream into blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into a cup and enjoy!
